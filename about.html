Steve Jobs co-founded Apple Inc. in 1976 with Steve Wozniak, revolutionizing the tech industry with iconic products like the Apple I and II, and later, the Macintosh.

It all started in 1 April 1976 It’ll blow your mind.

On April 1, 1976, Apple Inc. was founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in Cupertino, California, marking the beginning of a journey that would lead to the company becoming one of the world's most influential technology giants

It All Started in Los Altos, California, United States It’ll blow your mind.

Apple's roots trace back to Los Altos, California, where Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak, working in Jobs' garage, began building the first Apple computer, the Apple I, in 1976. This suburban locale became the birthplace of a global technology empire.
